Pizza

Description

This is a recipe for brooklyn style pizza

Ingredients

  • Warm water
  • Dry active yeast
  • Cold water
  • Salt
  • Bread flour
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Toppings of your choice

Steps

  1. Make dough with water flour and salt and yeast
  2. Knead said dough
  3. Put the dough in the fridge for 16 hours
  4. Knead the dough flat and sprinkle it with your toppings
  5. Put into oven until the crust turn brown
  6. Done!
