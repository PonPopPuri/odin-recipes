Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe for homemade lasagna which will blow your tastebuds away!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
  6. Done!
