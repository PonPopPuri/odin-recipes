Instant Ramen

Description

This is a recipe for instant ramen, a cheap source of carbs and flavour!

Ingredients

  • Instant Ramen of your choice
  • Water
  • Some heat source

Steps

  1. Get a container for water
  2. Bring the amount of water as instructed on the package th a boil
  3. Drop the noodles into the boiling water
  4. Let it boil for a few minutes
  5. Turn off the heat source
  6. Add the provided seasoning
  7. Stir vigorously aaaand Done!
